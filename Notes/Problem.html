Problems

  • Most problems can be solved in a number of ways and it is not always clear which way is better
  • A problem solving stratergy provides a framework within which you are more likely to arrive at a good solution
  • It is foolish to answer a question that you do not understand

    • stages of problem solving

    1. Understand the problem
    2. Devise a plan to solve the problem
    3. Carry out a plan
    4. Look back (check the result and reflect up it)
    5. Describe what we have learned from the process
    6. Explain our results(and document our program)
