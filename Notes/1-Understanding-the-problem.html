Step 1: Understanding the Problem

  • The first task is to understand the problem
  • Some problems can be very easy to understand, others can be harder
  • We must become familiar with the language of the problem, read it several times and become aware of its principle parts
  • Some time rephrasing the problem or describing it with a different representation from the original can be useful
  • drawing a picture or a diagram may shed light on what is required
  • What do we know for sure
  • What is unknown
  • Look at the different parts of the problem and consider the individually
  • Look at the individual parts and see how the relate to each other
  • What effect would a change in one aspect have on the other parts
