Panagrams are holoalphabetic - words or sentances that contain every letter in the alphabet Here is a famous one used by computers to show how text looks in different forms the quick brown dog jumps over the lazy dog Panagrams are useful in digitial typography because they demonstrate all the letters in a font withing a more meaningful context then just writing the alaphabet The perfect panagram is isogrammatic, that is is uses each letter only once Here are some more panagrams with their letter count shown in parenthesis Pack my box with fice dozen liquor jugs (32 e,i and o are repeated) waltz, bad nymph for quick jigs vex (only a and i are repeated but not as meaningful) siz plump boys guzzled cheap raw vodka quite joyfully Quick waxy bugs jump the frozen veldt Brick quiz whangs jumpy veldt fox
